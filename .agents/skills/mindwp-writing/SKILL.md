# MindWP Writing

MindWP writing starts with the buyer's working day, then names the website system and connected handling. It should sound observed, practical, calm, and commercially serious.

## Core Pattern

Write:

```text
flow -> handling -> result
```

Do not default to:

```text
pages -> design -> features
tools -> services -> features
```

Reach the concrete offer and real proof quickly.

## Guardrails

- No fake proof: no invented numbers, rankings, ratings, reviews, client names, locations, results, or guarantees.
- Do not name backend platforms or white-label delivery names publicly.
- Do not frame MindWP as SaaS, AI chatbot, CRM reseller, ads agency, SEO hype, quote tool, or invoice platform.
- Do not write "your CRM" or imply the client owns/runs the handling system.
- Attach ownership language to the website, domain, content, enquiries, work, and data.
- MindWP does not create, prepare, price, invoice, automatically send, or send quotes.
- Clinics are practice-front-door pages, not medical software, EMR, compliance, or treatment-claim pages.

## Service Boundaries

- Smart Website Systems owns website clarity, trust, conversion structure, enquiry paths, and the website-to-handling connection.
- Local SEO owns find/verify/local trust, not ranking guarantees.
- Lead Response & Handling owns first response and routing, not full CRM lifecycle.
- Follow-Up & CRM owns ownership, status, reminders, and next steps after first response or quote exists.
- Reputation & Review owns review timing, feedback routing, and visible proof, not fake/bought/manipulated reviews.
- Implementation pages are pathways under Smart Website Systems.

## Language To Prefer

- calls, forms, messages, bookings, appointments, quotes, consultations
- enquiry lands somewhere useful
- the right person knows
- response path
- owner, status, next step
- follow-up does not depend on memory
- good work becomes proof
- nearby customers can find and verify you

## Language To Avoid

- leverage, streamline, seamless, robust, holistic, ecosystem
- all-in-one platform
- AI chatbot as the offer
- CRM platform setup as the product
- dominate Google
- guaranteed leads, revenue, rankings, reviews, or conversion lift
- cheap website packages

## CTA And Proof

- Primary CTA: `Request a Website Review`.
- Secondary path when useful: `See the work`.
- Strong close should usually offer one clear action.
- Proof labels are for evidence that could be mistaken for real client work or measured outcomes.
- Obvious constructed service-page artifacts do not need defensive labels.
